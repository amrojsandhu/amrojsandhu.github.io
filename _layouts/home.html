Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't the beta free?

We'd love to offer Readmo for free, but the high-quality text-to-speech voices that make it so effective are powered by TTS APIs that carry real costs per character read. Even during beta, every sentence you listen to incurs an API charge on our end. Charging a small fee lets us keep the servers running, maintain voice quality, and continue building features—without cutting corners on the listening experience.
